Scottish Fold cats are known for their unique folded ears, giving them an adorable, owl-like appearance. This charming breed, originating from Scotland, is friendly, affectionate, and loves human company. Their soft, dense coats and round eyes add to their appeal. Scottish Folds are playful yet calm, making them great companions for families or individuals. They often enjoy lounging in quirky positions, adding a touch of whimsy to any home.
